FloodSafe Philippines

A student-led CIV300 group dedicated to spreading awareness and addressing the impacts of flooding in the Philippines

Our Objectives

Many areas in the Philippines with tropical river catchments frequently experience flash floods, intense rainfalls, and typhoons [Journal Article]. Just recently, Typhoon Fung-Wong devastated the Philippines as the second typhoon in one week, causing flooding that killed at least six individuals, as well as over 1.4 million people to evacuate, showing how vulnerable the country’s infrastructure is to natural disasters [Recent Flooding]. Our mission is to raise awareness of the flooding in Philippines by observing how inefficient infrastructure and the lack of professional responsibility heavily impact environmental and social sustainability.
